Multimodal multiscale atlas

A multimodal description of brain development during folding

We will acquire and map data from our mammalian model systems combining our methods and expertise. In collaboration between our 4 labs, we will generate an unprecedented multimodal description of brain development during folding.

Preliminary data

Some preliminary data has already been obtained.

Neuronal migration in mouse, ferret and human

Victor Borrell & Laurent Nguyen

We started comparing the migration of mouse, ferret and human neurons in a collaboration between the labs of Victor Borrell and Laurent Nguyen.

Development of cortical connectivity in ferrets

Roberto Toro & Victor Borrell

We have collected first data on the development of the ferret connectome in collaboration between the labs of Roberto Toro and Victor Borrell.

Micro- and macrostructure in folding ferret brains

Roberto Toro & Victor Borrell

We can further integrate histological and MRI data of the ferret brain collected in previous projects in the labs of Roberto Toro and Victor Borrell.
